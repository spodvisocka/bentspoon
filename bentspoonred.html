I like this story a lot because I think it showcases how I channel one of my weaknesses into one of my biggest strengths: my impatience breeds creativity, hard work, and an independent development of my skills.

Non-Linear

I'm a deeply non-linear thinker. While sometimes, A to B to C truly is the shortest way forward, often it's something else. Something closer to A to

to 24.

I'm not afraid of ways of thinking that seem to be nonsensical at first. My Master's thesis was about the agency and multiple intelligences of mushrooms. That's not something you cover in a linear sense. That's something you start in Tassili-n-Ajjer in 12,000 BC and end in the modern ethnography of the nightclub.

And so why is this an asset to you? Communications aren't linear. To strengthen the image and the brand, you can't talk the same content through the same form and expect results. You need different timing, angles, mediums, tones, dialects. You need to push an idea as a whole.

That holstic, encompassing approach is the true benefit of non-linearity. And it's something I have built-in.
